Who are we?

We are Thai Cottage. A proudly local Thai Cuisine Restaurant, with a passion for delivering authentic Thai flavors, we strive to provide an exceptional dining experience to our customers. Our team of talented chefs meticulously crafts each dish, ensuring a perfect blend of traditional Thai ingredients and modern culinary techniques.

Our Experience.

With over 10 years of culinary expertise, Thai Cottage has become synonymous with exceptional Thai cuisine. Our commitment to delivering authentic flavors and an unforgettable dining experience has made us a trusted name in the industry. Join us as we continue to elevate the art of Thai cooking and embark on a journey of taste and tradition.

Our Location.

Nestled in the vibrant suburb of Cranbourne, Melbourne, Thai Cottage stands as a culinary gem in the heart of the city. With a prime location, we bring the authentic flavors of Thailand right to your doorstep. Situated amidst the bustling streets and vibrant energy of Cranbourne, our restaurant offers a convenient and inviting space for locals and visitors alike to savor the finest Thai cuisine.
